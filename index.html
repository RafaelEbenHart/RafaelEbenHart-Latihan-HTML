ARTIC MONKEY

Profile

Arctic Monkeys were formed in mid-2002 by friends Alex Turner, Matt Helders, and Andy Nicholson

(who left the band shortly after their debut album, Whatever People Say I Am, That's What I'm Not, was released).

Turner and Helders were neighbours and close friends, and they met Nicholson in secondary school.
